Tangram Plus

2021-02-15 Logic

Nine Tangram Puzzles.
Including Chinese classic Tangram and 15 pieces puzzle(Yi Zhi Tu), as well as T puzzle, Heart-shaped puzzle, Pythagoras, Sphinx and Modern Tangram.

A. About Tangram
Tangram is China's most famous puzzle. In the early nineteenth century tangram became the first international puzzle. There are many tangram style puzzle games in the world.

B. About This Game
This game contains nine types of Tangram puzzle. Games and levels are constantly added. Tangram puzzles include the Chinese classic Tangram and 15 pieces puzzle(Yi Zhi Tu), as well as T puzzle, Heart-shaped puzzle, Pythagoras, Sphinx and Modern Tangram(Lou Zhuqiu). There are three kind of play methods. Not just move. You can play classic game(Move, Flip & Rotate) or for relaxing(Just Move).

Features:

  1. Nine Types of tangram Puzzle.
  2. Three Play Methods.
  3. Over 2100 Levels and Will Add More.
  4. Easy to Play.
  5. Five Themes.

C. How to Play

  1. This game have Three Play Methods.
    Just Move.
    Move & Flip.
    Move, Flip & Rotate.
  2. Move: Touch the Piece and move.
    Flip: Click the Piece.
    Rotate:Long touch the Piece.
  3. Get Hint: Click the Hint Button. Each Level has 2 hints.
